Types of loops in PL/SQL
1. Simple loops
2. While loop
3. For loop

SYNTAX
SIMPLE LOOP
	loop
	<set of statements>
	exit when <condition>;
	<set of statements>
	end loop;

	or	


	loop
	<set of statements>
	if <condition> then <operation>;
	<set of statements>
	exit;
	end if;
	<set of statements>
	end loop;
